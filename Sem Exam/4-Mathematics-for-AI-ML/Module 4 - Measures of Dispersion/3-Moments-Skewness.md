Measure of asymmetry.
- **Symmetrical**: Mean = Median = Mode (Skew = 0).
- **Positively Skewed (Right)**: Tail on right. Mean > Median.
- **Negatively Skewed (Left)**: Tail on left. Mean < Median.

## ğŸ“Œ 3. Kurtosis
Measure of "tailedness" or peakness.
- **Mesokurtic**: Normal distribution (Kurtosis = 3).
- **Leptokurtic**: Sharp peak, fat tails (Kurtosis > 3).
- **Platykurtic**: Flat peak, thin tails (Kurtosis < 3).
